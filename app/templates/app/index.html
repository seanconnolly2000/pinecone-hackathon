Based upon the role you logged in as, you will have access to different functions (which control data sources) shown the tiles above (APIs,Vector Databases, SQL Databases). ChatCompletion will determine what functions it needs to execute to answer your question based upon the Access Control List of functions for your role. If you are using a SQL database call, ChatCompletion is creating the SELECT statement based upon the data table schema it is being supplied! Due to this, we occasionally see "I don't have access to data" issues when ChatCompletion gets the SQL incorrect. Incorrect SQL statements are logged in DocumentDB for analysis and tweaking of table structures (for instance, introducing a SQL View to simplify complex table joins).

  • What is my name and what time is it? (internal getUTC function)
  • What will the weather be in San Francisco tomorrow? (WeatherAPI)
  • Please give me news on the US Economy (NewsAPI)
  • Are eye glasses covered in my Northwinds Standard Benefits Plan? (Pinecone)
  • What were our sales in 2018? (SQL sales role)
  • What are the departments within our company? (Sql HR Role, However, ChatGPT may try to get a distinct list from sales - it's pretty resourceful!)
